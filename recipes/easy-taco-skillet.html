Easy Taco Skillet

Easy taco skillet bake that uses only one pan. This recipe can be made for under $10!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup converted rice
  • 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce

Directions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat
  2. cook and stir beef and onion until beef is browned, about 5 minutes
  3. Drain.
  4. Stir tomatoes, water, rice, and taco seasoning into beef mixture and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender, about 25 minutes.
  6. Top with Mexican cheese blend and lettuce before serving.
